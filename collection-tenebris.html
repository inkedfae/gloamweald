Tenebris

Keep one small light where the dark cannot reach it.

☾

The city below dusk

When the lamps went low.

No one remembers whether the sun abandoned Tenebris or the city simply sank beneath it. Its people learned to carry their light in smaller things: a stone, a relic, a glint of metal beneath a sleeve.

These pieces hold fragments rather than answers—fossil, gemstone, and steel arranged like keepsakes from a place that only exists after nightfall.
