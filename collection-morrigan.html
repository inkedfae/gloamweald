The Morrigan

Some warnings arrive on black wings.

III

An omen in three parts

At the field's black edge.

The first feather was found at the boundary stone. The second lay across the threshold. The third was waiting inside.

The Morrigan is a collection of dark points, pale fragments, and repeated threes. Each piece is made for the moment when instinct speaks before certainty—and you decide to listen.
