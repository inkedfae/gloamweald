Materials & care

Stainless steel, treated kindly.

Sturdy does not mean indestructible. A little care keeps the rings moving cleanly.

01

Clean

Use mild soap and lukewarm water. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners.

02

Dry

Dry thoroughly with a soft cloth, paying attention to water caught between rings.

03

Store

Keep pieces dry and untangled. Store bone, fossil, and gemstone components with extra care.

04

Check

If a ring opens or a finding shifts, stop wearing the piece and contact Gloamweald for advice.
