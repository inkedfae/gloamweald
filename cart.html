Small length adjustments

Most made-to-order Gloamweald pieces can have up to 2 cm added or removed at no extra cost.

If you would like a small adjustment, include the item name and requested change in the order notes, for example:

“Dark Elf Bracelet +1.5 cm”

or

“Small Half-Persian Necklace -2 cm”

Requests over 2 cm, major sizing changes, or design changes should be discussed by email or Instagram before ordering.

More about delivery and shipping

All Gloamweald orders are sent through Australia Post with tracking.

Standard tracked shipping within Australia — $10.95

Express tracked shipping within Australia — $13.95

Standard tracked shipping is free on Australian orders of $150 or more. An express upgrade is available for an additional $3.

Most Gloamweald pieces are made to order or finished to size before dispatch. Tracking details will be emailed once your order has been posted.

Gloamweald is an online-only studio and does not currently offer local pickup as a standard checkout option. If you would like to discuss pickup, please contact Gloamweald by email or Instagram before placing your order.
